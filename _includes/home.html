✳

Sharism is the philosophy of Sharing. You can read about the idea on Wikipedia, or read the essay that started it all. We have also organized some events, workshops, and a forum.

Agree?

Whenever someone marks a work with ✳, it means that person is willing to share it with you.

The work can be used worldwide for any purpose whatsoever (no copyright), so you're free to copy, use, and redistribute the work any way you like. Giving credit is optional.

If you need more clarity, please read the Sharing Agreement. It's lengthy, but legit.

Share

To share your own work with the Sharing Agreement, simply write "✳ Your Name" like so. You can link the ✳ to the Agreement, and link your name to the work you are sharing.

If you need help formatting this for the web, simply type your name and the URL to your work into the form below, then copy the HTML onto your own website.
